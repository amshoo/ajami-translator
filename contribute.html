🎙️ RECORDING QUALITY TIPS

  • Use a phone or laptop microphone in a quiet room
  • Say the word once, naturally — not too slow or exaggerated
  • Avoid starting with breath noise — pause 0.5s before speaking
  • If you make a mistake, just click Record again
  • Native Hausa speakers preferred, but all contributions welcome
