What is Ajami?

Ajami is the practice of writing African languages using Arabic script. Hausa speakers have used it since the 17th century for poetry, trade, and Islamic scholarship.

🔄

Two directions

Translate English → Hausa Ajami to write, or Ajami → English to read and understand historical Hausa manuscripts and documents.

🔊

Audio

Click the speaker button to hear Hausa spoken. Audio quality improves as community members contribute recordings to the dictionary.
